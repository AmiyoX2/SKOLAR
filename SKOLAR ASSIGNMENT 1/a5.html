Here are my top 5 moives:

  1. CASABLANCA
    • Year of Release: 1942
    • Main Actors: Humphrey Bogart, Ingrid Bergman, Paul Henreid
    • Description: Directed by Michael Curtiz, Casablanca is a classic romance set during World War II. The film centers on Rick Blaine, a nightclub owner in Casablanca, who must choose between his love for Ilsa Lund and helping her husband, a resistance leader, escape from the Nazis.
  2. THE LORD OF THE RINGS: THE FELLOWSHIP OF THE RING
    • Year of Release: 2001
    • Main Actors: Elijah Wood, Ian McKellen, Viggo Mortensen
    • Description: Directed by Peter Jackson, this fantasy epic is the first film in The Lord of the Rings trilogy. Based on J.R.R. Tolkien's novel, the movie follows Frodo Baggins as he embarks on a perilous journey to destroy the One Ring and prevent the dark lord Sauron from conquering Middle-earth.
  3. THE DARK KNIGHT
    • Year of Release: 2008
    • Main Actors: Christian Bale, Heath Ledger, Aaron Eckhart
    • Description: Directed by Christopher Nolan, The Dark Knight is a superhero film that explores the conflict between Batman and the Joker. Set in Gotham City, the film delves into themes of chaos, morality, and justice, with Heath Ledger's performance as the Joker earning widespread acclaim.
  4. TITANIC
    • Year of Release: 1997
    • Main Actors: Leonardo DiCaprio, Kate Winslet, Billy Zane
    • Description: Directed by James Cameron, Titanic is a romance-disaster film that tells the tragic love story of Jack and Rose, two passengers from different social classes who meet aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic. The movie combines epic scale with intimate drama, leading to its status as one of the highest-grossing films of all time.
  5. INCEPTION
    • Year of Release: 2010
    • Main Actors: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page
    • Description: Directed by Christopher Nolan, Inception is a science fiction thriller that delves into the world of shared dreaming. The film follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from within the subconscious, as he attempts a seemingly impossible task: planting an idea in someone's mind.
